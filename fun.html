We are Teaggs

We are a new established brunch brand located in the middle of the city of Oslo in Norway.

We are a new established business that usually serves brunch consisting of tea and eggs at brunch time. And you can get this experience for just a small amount of money. When it comes to serving tea and eggs we are the best in Norway, and maybe even the world!
